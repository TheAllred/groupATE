ILO #3: Effective Communicators

Become better communicators by completing Pico Park

The goal is to prove that we are effective communicators by playing Pico Park to completion.

What is Pico Park?

Pico Park is a couch-co-op game that challenges the players with a series of puzzels that are only possible to complete if all members of the team are working together.

Takeaways:

Talk it out first. You save more time by taking the time to establish a clear plan where everyone is on the same page.
